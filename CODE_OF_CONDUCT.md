# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume positive intent and provide actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions evidence-based and focused on technical outcomes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after moderator intervention.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict
participation for behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

For conduct concerns, contact **contact@synapticfour.com**.
