# EU AI Act (Articles 10 & 11) Mapping

HELIOS contributes to technical documentation and data governance obligations.

- **Article 10 (Data and data governance)**:
  - Tracks reference genome provenance and variant interpretation quality markers.
  - Preserves immutable input/output hashes to support dataset lineage.
- **Article 11 (Technical documentation)**:
  - Produces signed records of pipeline settings, software context, and checks.
  - Exports human-readable and machine-readable reports suitable for conformity files.
